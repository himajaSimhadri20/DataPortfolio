Data-Driven-Insights-into-Learning-Platforms.

This portfolio showcases my ability to leverage Power BI to analyze the performance of an online learning platform. I meticulously cleaned and transformed the raw data, handling missing values and ensuring data consistency. I then designed and implemented a data model, establishing relationships between tables to enable comprehensive analysis.Using DAX measures, I calculated key performance indicators (KPIs) such as "Total Revenue," "Average Pricing," "Average Time Effort," and "Average Course Length." I created a range of visualizations, including pie charts, bar charts, and text boxes, to effectively communicate data insights and trends.


Key Insights:

• Strong User Base: The platform boasts a substantial user base of 45 million students, indicating a significant market presence.
• Diverse Course Offerings: The platform offers a wide range of courses across various subjects, catering to diverse learning interests.
• Revenue Generation: The platform generates significant revenue, demonstrating its financial success and the value it provides to both students and instructors.
• Content Diversity: The breakdown of courses by level (Introductory, Intermediate, Advanced) indicates a well-rounded content strategy to cater to learners at different skill levels.

Technical Skills Demonstrated:

• Data Cleaning and Transformation
• Data Modeling
• DAX Measures
• Data Visualization
• Dashboard Design
• Data Analysis and Interpretation

Summary

This project demonstrates my ability to leverage Power BI to analyze complex data, extract meaningful insights, and create compelling visualizations that effectively communicate key performance indicators and trends. The dashboard provides a valuable tool for stakeholders to understand platform performance and make data-driven decisions to optimize the learning experience for students.
