Analyzing Udemy Course Performance with Power BI

This portfolio showcases my ability to leverage Power BI to analyze Udemy course data, extract key insights, and create compelling visualizations

Technical Skills Demonstrated:

• Data Cleaning and Transformation: I meticulously cleaned and transformed the raw Udemy data, handling missing values, formatting data types, and creating new calculated columns (e.g., "Total Revenue") using DAX.
• Data Modeling: I designed and implemented a data model, including creating a calendar table and establishing relationships between tables (e.g., between the date dimension and the course publication date) to enable dynamic date-based analysis.
• DAX Measures: I developed and utilized DAX measures to calculate key performance indicators (KPIs) such as "Total Revenue," "Year-over-Year Growth" for various metrics (Subscribers, Lectures, Revenue, Reviews), and other relevant calculations.
• Data Visualization: I created a range of visualizations including stacked area charts, bar charts, scatter plots, and dynamic cards to effectively communicate data insights and trends.
• Interactive Dashboards: I built interactive dashboards with filters (e.g., "FREE" and "PAID" courses) and dynamic elements to enable users to explore the data and gain deeper insights.

Analysis and Insights:

• Overall Growth: The 2011-2017 dashboard revealed strong overall growth across key metrics like subscribers, lectures, and revenue. However, the 2015-2017 dashboard highlights a slowdown in growth rates, particularly in subscribers and revenue. This suggests a need to analyze the factors contributing to this deceleration.
• Content Expansion: The continued increase in lectures across both time periods demonstrates a commitment to expanding the course library. However, analyzing the quality and relevance of new content is crucial to ensure its impact on user engagement and revenue.
• User Engagement: The analysis revealed a positive trend in user engagement, as evidenced by the increase in reviews. However, the decline in student reviews in the 2015-2017 period warrants further investigation to understand the underlying reasons.
• Subject Performance: The focus on Web Development in the second dashboard provides valuable insights into the performance of this specific subject area. Analyzing factors such as course popularity, student enrollment, and revenue generated for Web Development courses can inform strategic decisions and content development.

Key Takeaways:
• The analysis highlights the importance of tracking key performance indicators and monitoring trends over time.
• The use of data visualization techniques effectively communicates complex data and insights to stakeholders.
• Continuous analysis and adaptation are crucial for navigating the dynamic online learning landscape and ensuring sustained platform growth
