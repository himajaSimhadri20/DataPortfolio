Decoding Global Fitness Trends: A Data Analysis Exploration

As a data analyst, I embarked on a project to understand the evolving landscape of global fitness trends. Leveraging Python and its powerful data science libraries, I analyzed a collection of datasets provided by datacamp. These datasets, including workout.csv, three_keywords.csv, and workout_geo.csv offered invaluable insights into the popularity of various fitness-related keywords on a global scale.

My analysis focused on several key objectives:

1. Identifying Global Workout Trends: By analyzing the "workout.csv" dataset, I visualized the global search volume for "workout" over time using matplotlib.
This visualization revealed the year of peak interest, providing valuable insights into the overall trajectory of global fitness awareness.
2. Comparing Keyword Popularity: I delved into the "three_keywords.csv" dataset to understand the evolving popularity of "home workout," "gym workout," and "home gym." Utilizing pandas,
I identified the year of peak interest for each keyword, shedding light on the shifting preferences within the fitness industry.
3. Analyzing Regional Variations: The "workout_geo.csv" and "three_keywords_geo.csv" datasets allowed me to explore regional variations in workout interest. By analyzing these datasets,
I identified the country with the highest overall workout interest and further investigated regional preferences for different workout types.

Technical Skills:
• Data Loading and Manipulation: Proficiently used pandas to read CSV files, clean and prepare data for analysis, and perform data transformations.
• Data Visualization: Employed matplotlib to create insightful visualizations, effectively communicating data trends and patterns.
• Data Analysis: Utilized Python's capabilities for data analysis, including filtering, indexing, and identifying key metrics (peak values, maximum values).
Key Insights:
• Peak global workout interest occurred in [2020]
• Home workouts saw a significant increase in popularity during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Key Takeaways:
This project provided me with a valuable opportunity to apply my data analysis skills to a real-world scenario. I gained a deeper understanding of global fitness trends and the power of data-driven insights in understanding consumer behavior. This project has further strengthened my proficiency in Python and its data science libraries, equipping me with valuable skills for future data analysis endeavors.
